Before the internet sucked, people used to make their own websites...no ads and no subscriptions - just for fun!

The INTERBONG is an independent library of intriguing links to lesser known sites or artists. This content below is not being promoted to you, nor generated by some tech bro's algorithms trying to "build engagement" or "maximize revenues" -- but rather is a human-curated selection of some of my favorite websites. No ads, no trackers, no analytics, no sponsors, no logins, no revenue, no crap.

Some of these are long gone from active hosting, but where possible the Wayback Machine link is still posted.
